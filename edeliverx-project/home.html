About Us

At eDeliverX, we specialize in helping electronic product retailers make a seamless transition to online platforms using Amazon Web Services (AWS). Our goal is to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of web app functionality and IT infrastructure while ensuring scalability and flexibility even during peak demand periods like Black Friday and Christmas.

With our expertise in e-commerce and AWS, we offer tailored solutions that empower you to offer delivery facilities as part of your products. Our focus on leveraging AWS ensures a scalable and reliable infrastructure, while our app simplifies and enhances your delivery services.

We pride ourselves on our customer-centric approach, aligning our solutions with the needs of your customer, marketing, and logistics departments. By exclusively utilizing AWS, we provide a secure and scalable foundation for your online platform.

Join us at eDeliverX and unlock the full potential of your electronic retail business online. Let's create an exceptional shopping experience and drive your business growth together.
